#SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

This is a very simple school System that has students , teachers , admin and courses.

#Student
Students can log into the system and register and can choose the courses that interest them after browsing through them and apply for registration.

#teacher
Teacher registers into the System and can enter the courses they can offer and wait for admin to assign them to the courses they choose.
They can also view through all the kids in their course and their grades in their course.
Can delete update and add a student into the system.

#admin
Admin confirms teachers registration and also the students registration and can see all the students and teachers in the system.
Views all the course in the System and is able to add , update and deleted a course from the system.
Also deletes adds and updates both the students and teachers in the System.
